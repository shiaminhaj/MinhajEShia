Privacy Policy

Effective: March 2026 · Last updated: March 2026

No Personal Data
We do not collect names, emails, or identifying information.
Offline First
All content lives on your device. Nothing leaves your phone.
Zero Tracking
No ads, no analytics profiles, no behavioral tracking.

Short version: Minhaj e Shia is a fully offline app. We do not operate servers, do not collect personal data, and do not sell or share any information. The only data that ever leaves your device is anonymous crash diagnostics, and only if the app crashes.

01Who We Are

Minhaj e Shia ("the App") is a free, open-source Islamic companion application available on Android, iOS, and Windows. It is developed and maintained by a small team of volunteers. There is no company, no subscription, and no revenue model — the App is and will always be free.

For questions about this policy, contact us at shiaminhaj@gmail.com.

02Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any contact information
  • An account — there is no sign-up or login
  • Your reading history, bookmarks, or prayer activity
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID, etc.)
  • IP addresses or network metadata
  • Location history
  • Payment or financial information
  • Cookies or cross-site tracking data

03Location Data

The App requests your device location solely to calculate accurate Islamic prayer times. This computation happens entirely on-device.

  • Your coordinates are never transmitted to any server or third party
  • Coordinates are not stored beyond the current session unless you explicitly save a preferred location
  • The App works without location permission — you may enter your city manually
  • On Android, background location may be requested for home screen widget refresh; this data is processed locally

04Crash Reports (Sentry)

When the App crashes, it sends an anonymous diagnostic report to Sentry containing:

  • The error message and stack trace
  • Device type and OS version
  • App version number
  • Approximate timezone

These reports do not contain personal data or any information that could identify you. See sentry.io/privacy.

05Local Storage

The App stores data on your device using SQLite databases:

  • All Quran, Hadith, Duas, Amaal, and Calendar content (bundled at install)
  • Your bookmarks, favorites, and reading progress
  • App settings and preferences
  • Downloaded azan audio files

None of this is backed up to our servers. Uninstalling removes all data.

06Permissions Requested

  • Location — prayer time calculation (optional)
  • Notifications — azan alarms and prayer reminders (optional)
  • Storage / Media — caching downloadable azan audio
  • Exact Alarm (Android 12+) — azan timing accuracy
  • Foreground Service (Android) — reliable azan playback

All permissions are optional and can be revoked at any time.

07Third-Party Services

The only third-party service is Sentry for crash reporting (§4). Azan audio is fetched from raw.githubusercontent.com, governed by GitHub's Privacy Statement.

08Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and collects no information from anyone. It inherently complies with COPPA and similar regulations.

09Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing to provide, correct, or delete. All user data exists only on your device. Contact shiaminhaj@gmail.com with questions — we respond within 30 days.

10Changes to This Policy

Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date and communicated via the App. We will never introduce data collection or advertising without prominent in-app notification.
