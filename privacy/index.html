Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026  ·  Last updated: March 2026

No Personal Data
We do not collect names, emails, or any identifying information.
Offline First
All content lives on your device. Nothing leaves your phone.
Zero Tracking
No ads, no analytics profiles, no behavioral tracking.

Short version: Minhaj e Shia is a fully offline app. We do not operate servers, do not collect personal data, and do not sell or share any information. The only data that ever leaves your device is anonymous crash diagnostics, and only if the app crashes.

01Who We Are

Minhaj e Shia ("the App") is a free, open-source Islamic companion application available on Android, iOS, and Windows. It is developed and maintained by a small team of volunteers. There is no company, no subscription, and no revenue model — the App is and will always be free.

For any questions about this policy, contact us at shiaminhaj@gmail.com.

02Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any contact information
  • An account — there is no sign-up or login
  • Your reading history, bookmarks, or prayer activity
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID, etc.)
  • IP addresses or network metadata
  • Location history
  • Payment or financial information
  • Cookies or cross-site tracking data

03Location Data

The App requests your device location solely to calculate accurate Islamic prayer times for your current coordinates. This computation happens entirely on-device using a local algorithm.

  • Your coordinates are never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Your coordinates are not stored beyond the current app session unless you explicitly save them as a preferred location
  • The App works without location permission — you may enter your city manually instead
  • On Android, the App may request background location to refresh prayer times for home screen widgets; this data is processed locally by the system and never leaves the device

04Crash Reports (Sentry)

When the App crashes, it sends an anonymous diagnostic report to Sentry to help us identify and fix bugs. These reports contain:

  • The error message and stack trace
  • Device type and OS version (e.g., "Android 14", "iPhone 13")
  • App version number
  • Approximate timezone (not precise location)

These reports do not contain personal data, user content, or any information that could identify you. Sentry's privacy policy is available at sentry.io/privacy.

Crash reporting can be disabled in App Settings → Privacy.

05Local Storage

The App stores data on your device using SQLite databases and system storage. This includes:

  • All Quran, Hadith, Duas, Amaal, and Calendar content (bundled at install)
  • Your bookmarks, favorites, and reading progress (stored locally only)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Downloaded azan audio files (if you select a downloadable reciter)

None of this local data is backed up to our servers. Uninstalling the App removes all local data.

06Permissions Requested

  • Location — for prayer time calculation (optional; you can enter city manually)
  • Notifications — for azan alarms and prayer reminders (optional)
  • Storage / Media — for caching downloadable azan audio files
  • Exact Alarm (Android 12+) — required for azan alarms to fire on time
  • Foreground Service (Android) — for reliable azan playback

All permissions are optional and can be revoked at any time via your device Settings. Revoking location permission simply means prayer times must be set manually.

07Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media SDKs. The only third-party service used is:

  • Sentry — anonymous crash reporting (described in §4 above)

Azan audio files for downloadable reciters are fetched from GitHub's content delivery network (raw.githubusercontent.com), which is governed by GitHub's Privacy Statement. No personal data is sent in these requests.

08Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from children. Because we collect no personal data at all, the App inherently complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and similar regulations.

09Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing for us to provide, correct, or delete on your behalf. All user-generated content (bookmarks, settings, preferences) exists only on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the App or clearing its data from device Settings.

If you have questions about data you believe we may hold, contact us at shiaminhaj@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

10Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and, where appropriate, notify users via the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

We will never introduce data collection, advertising, or user tracking without a prominent in-app notification and an updated policy.

11Contact

shiaminhaj@gmail.com

We are a volunteer team — please allow up to 30 days for a response.
